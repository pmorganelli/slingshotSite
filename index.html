About the Game

Slingshot Squires combines the strategic tower defense of Plants vs. Zombies with the physics-based action of Angry Birds, all set in a charming pixel-art world inspired by Stardew Valley. Defend your farm from waves of mischievous creatures using your trusty slingshot and strategic placement of defensive structures.
