Slingshot Squires

Defend your farm with strategic slingshot action!

About the Game

Slingshot Squires combines the strategic tower defense of Plants vs. Zombies with the physics-based action of Angry Birds, all set in a charming pixel-art world inspired by Stardew Valley.

Strategic Defense

Place and upgrade defensive structures to protect your farm.

Precise Combat

Master the click-and-drag slingshot mechanics against hungry pests.

Farm Life

Grow crops and financially benefit the Squire. Survive as many rounds as you can!

Characters

The Squire

Your main character, skilled in both farming and combat. He's your go-to for everything Slingshot Squires!

The Mosquito

Common enemy. Easy to kill. Don't let them pile up!

The Worm

A quicker, less common enemy. Worms love to wreak havoc on your crops.

The Slime

The biggest enemy in the game. Encountered after wave 7. Slimes are chunky, so kill them before they one-hit your crops!

How to Play

1. Purchase Your Crops

Strategically use your coins to protect your farm and yield the most profit.

2. Aim and Fire

Use your slingshot to take down enemies with precise shots and defend your precious goods.

3. Upgrade and Progress

Earn coins by killing enemies to upgrade your crops and unlock new ammunition.
